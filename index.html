About

Vera Szeto has lived in the Bay Area for 32 years, and has a background in business, accounting, education, and culture.

She was born in Shanghai and has seen this small city transform into a world-class metropolis. Vera speaks English, Shanghainese, Mandarin and Cantonese. Because of Veraâ€™s diverse background, she is able to relate to people from all walks of life. Along her own life journey, she found herself drawn to finding homes for friends, and friends of friends. She wanted to see people placed in areas that fit their needs and lifestyles.

In addition to her affinity for Real Estate, Vera is also a classical pianist. For her, Music stirs up the imagination and carries her dreams. Her desire is to find a home that brings the new owners that same excitement. A home is not simply a place of residence. Your home should promote relationships and happiness, and with happiness, dreams are created and realized. She herself is a dreamer: her dreams brought her from Shanghai to SF to Berkeley, to Piedmont, and to being a wife and mother. As a Real Estate professional, she wants to help you to find your next home. Please call or email Vera Szeto using the contact info below.
